The Dresden Files

  1. Dresden Files Characters in First Book
    1. Harry Dresden



      1. Powerful Wizard
      2. Always manages to get himself into trouble.
      3. Private investigator/Warden, Regional Commander of eastern parts of North America.
    2. Bob



      1. Air elemental (spirit of intellect).
      2. Resides in a human skull owned by Harry.
    3. Karrin Murphy



      1. Sergeant in the Chicago PD assigned to Special Investigations.r
      2. Harry often works with her on "unusual" cases.
  2. Characters introduced in other books in the series
    1. Molly Carpenter



      1. High-school student, oldest child of Michael and Charity Carpenter, and as of Proven Guilty, apprentice to "Harry Dresden."
    2. Mouse



      1. Caucasian Shepherd Dog or Tibetan Mastiff, a descendant of a divine guardian spirit and a mortal canine.
      2. A temple dog, also called a Foo dog, but Harry calls him a cross between a chow and a woolly mammoth, and a "West Highland Dogosaurus."
    3. Michael Carpenter



      1. Former Knight of the Cross (sometimes called by others, Knight of the Sword).
      2. Father of Molly Carpenter.
    4. Gentleman "Johnny" Marcone



      1. Businessman, mob boss, Free Lord of the Unseelie Accords.
      2. A powerful crime boss who is an antagonist of Dresden's.
